About Me

My name is Clinton Thompson.

I grew up in Fort Myers, FL, and after finishing high school, decided to join the Marine Corps. While it wasn't the most enjoyable five years I've ever had, I was fortunate enough to meet some of the greatest people I'll ever know.

After the leaving the Marine Corps, and taking a short break, I enlisted in the Army for an additional five years.

Deciding at the end of that contract that I was finished with military life, I decided to pursue a career in web development. I enrolled in devCodeCamp's full stack developer course, which layed the foundation for my continued self study.

I make sure to carve out time each day to continue learning, and look for ways to improve my skills as a developer

In addition to software development, I am currently studying for the Comptia A+, to improve my all around IT skills. I am grateful for the opportunity to learn, and I plan to continue developing my skills, and learning new technologies.
